Robomate

The specific problem is the barrier to deploying robotics and automation for SMEs, enabling them to adapt to the growing labour shortage and reduce the labour cost per part. Our challenge is to reduce these barriers by making an accessible solution and thereby allowing SMEs to repurpose automation cells without robotic programming expertise. In turn, this will render our manufacturing sector more robust, amplifying the workers’ productivity adapting to the societal challenge of labour shortage and demographic changes.

Why Robomate?

Manufacturing Sector

Is critical in Ireland, contributing up to 37% of GDP and employing 12% of total working population

Undesirable Work

Roles that are physically demanding, repetitive with unsatisfactory working conditions and hence are unattractive for new workforce entrants.

Labour Shortage

There is a growing labour shortage in manufacturing with lack of both skilled and unskilled labour, particularly affecting SMEs and threatening their future success.

Solution Development

Software

The solution is a software application that connects to any collaborative robot (Cobot) and 3D vision system. The software enables a human to teach robots manufacturing operations in minutes.

Teaching

It reads the robot’s state and vision data during teaching and controls the robot’s motion based on vision feedback during execution.

Non-Experts

Through a single demonstration, a non-expert user can automate manufacturing tasks using this software much faster than classical approaches.
